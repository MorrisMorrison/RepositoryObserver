RepositoryObserver

Welcome to RepositoryObserver, a simple service to observe your GitHub repositories.

RepositoryObserver offers a service to observe your repositories for accidentally leaked vulnerable data like passwords or api-keys.
Simply authenticate with your Github account and select the repositories you want to observe.
The service allows you to specify different keywords and start frequent scans or scans on every commit.

How does this work?

RepositoryObserver offers a service to observe your repositories for accidentally leaked passwords.
Simply authenticate with your GitHub account and select the repositories you want to observe. Choose a frequency at which the scans are started or create a webhook to automatically scan on every commit.
