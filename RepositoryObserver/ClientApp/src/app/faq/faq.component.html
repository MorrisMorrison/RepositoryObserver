Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is this all about?

This service helps you not leaking any vulnerable information when pushing to public repos.

2. How does it work?

The service either scans your repositories every x minutes or it creates a repositoryhook and checks your repository every time any changes happen. When one of the specied keywords is found, a notification gets sent to you either via E-Mail, WhatsApp or SMS. Mobile messages are sent using Twilio.

3. Why does the app need permissions for GitHub?

Creating webhooks for repositories is only possible with full access to the account. Creating scheduled jobs only uses the public GitHub API and therefore is possible without full access.

4. Why is this shit so slow?

It is hosted on a free heroku dyno.

5. Are you going to collect my data?

Yes.
You can get a full dump of your data on the settings page.
